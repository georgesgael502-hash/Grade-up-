## What Is GradeUp?

GradeUp is a **zero-barrier academic toolkit** built for the 19 million students enrolled in US colleges and universities. It gives students instant answers during the moments that matter most:

- *"What grade do I need on my final to pass this class?"*
- *"What's my GPA if I get a B+ this semester?"*
- *"How do I structure the next 3 hours to actually finish this paper?"*

No account. No paywall. No ads interrupting mid-calculation. Just immediate, accurate help — which is exactly why students share it in group chats and Discord servers without being asked.
